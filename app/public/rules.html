Radius Cycling Team "rules"

General Classification

  • It's all about your miles.
  • Commuter miles get filtered. Be nice, come on a ride with the team.
  • If you ride with a team member, your miles are worth x1.5.

Climber Jersey

  • Total time on the following climbs:
    • Stinson to Pantoll (Category 2)
  • If you haven't done one of these Climbs you're not eligable for the Polka Dot.

Sprinter Jersey

  • Polo Field Lap (1 KM, 0.66mi)
  • No drafting
  • Only PRs of the current year

Radius Jersey

  • Hawk Hill, Hawk Hill, Hawk Hill
  • Only PRs of the current year
